SOLO

Creative Agency for Photographers

SOLO is a creative agency made for talented up and coming photographers. We have agents all across the world looking to find photographers who are inventive and inspiring when it comes to photography. We aspire to give our photographers full creative control whilst also providing them with equipment, studios and models etc. We work to help our photographers reach their full creative potential.
