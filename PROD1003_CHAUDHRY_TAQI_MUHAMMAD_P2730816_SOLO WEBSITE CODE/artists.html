Monique Yvonne Jones

Our first signee, Monique Yvonne Jones is a photographer who specialises in self portrait photography. She uses bright vivid colours to capture stunning cinematic portraits. She has garnered a large following and large amounts of success through social media platforms Instagram and TikTok (@moniqueyvonn). She also has a website in which she displays her portfolio containing her work, you can visit this website by clicking on the picture on the left.
